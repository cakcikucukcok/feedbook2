Do You Believe:

~ You are of value?

~ You are just a job description?

~ Your art of touch has a higher purpose?

In Healing Humanity from Behind the Shears you are challenged and encouraged to achieve a higher level of gratitude, awareness, and self-appreciation that will transcend you through clients; therefore, making our world a better place.

A wonderful read and lesson for everyone, not just for cosmetologists, on how to work at and be mindful everyday with the power of kindness and love that affects everyone we come into contact.
