Presenting a wake-up to the body of Christ, author Prophet Harrison I. Enudi seeks to help the children of God to discover their full spiritual ability and capacity in Christ in order to keep the devil under their control and be in charge of their destinies, spiritual environments, and all situations that emerge in their lives.

In Expressing the Supernatural, Enudi shows, through scriptural examples and quotes, how mankind can be delivered from fear of the kingdom of darkness and how they can regain their spiritual authority over Satan and all evil. He demonstrates the importance of being born again, the qualities of redemption, the three stages of Christianity, the spiritual factors needed for the possible expression of the supernatural, and the backbone for expressing the supernatural. He also explores how to become born again, how to live above sin, why the Holy Ghost is needed, and how to maintain the flow of the supernatural.

Expressing the Supernatural provides a guide to help believers access a transformation experience and be a true witness of Gods wisdom and power.
