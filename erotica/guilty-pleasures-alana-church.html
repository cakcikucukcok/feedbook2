As she prepares herself, she is haunted by memories of the past. Will the love of her new family sustain her? Or will she succumb to despair? Not because I don't love you,” she added, turning to face him and placing her hand on his cheek. He captured and held it there, then turned his head to kiss her palm. “But isn't what all of this is about? Learning to love, learning to give?” “Learning to receive love as well,” Maria added.
