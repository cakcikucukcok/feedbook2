A brilliant novel of mystery and history merged together when in 1891 a young girl disappears mysteriously and is never heard from again. Abigail Stone just graduated from college when she and her dog, Willow, are moving to St. Augustine, Florida, where she will be employed at the Blue Water Clinic. Shes rented a small home from a feisty sixty-eight-year-old lady who lives next door. Shortly after Willow and Abigail move in, Willow starts to investigate and finds a letter thats over one hundred years old. Abigail engages her landlord in the reading of the letter, and from there, they enter into a maze of danger, risk, and intrigue that begins when Henry Flagler and his second wife, Ida, were the talk of the town.
