For Larry and Laura, two lonely and different children, in one night they learn that their lives, up until now, have been nothing but a lie! And – their mother informs them of something so unbelievable, so life-changingly deceitful, that they are left completely gob smacked and stunned by what they hear!

Curiously, from that moment on, their world becomes so fantastical, so incredible and so utterly unbelievable that even in their wildest imaginings nothing could have prepared them for the things to come . . .

Welcome to The Advocates of Gaia – The Reaching Tower, the first novel about two children coming of age in a futuristic and dystopian society and a property where time and the grime of the world is kept out by the very fabric of the walls surrounding it.

A property where adventure is real and a part of everyday life and where people are strange, unusual and amazing; and are celebrated for being different. A place where earth energy and nature is revered, respected and embraced for all of future kind. A place the children finally feel at home at. A place where all things are possible and anything can and will happen.

A place where they will ultimately have to fight – fight for the very future of everything and everyone around them. A place where their protection and Ancestral legacy will be called into question and ultimately, they will have to fight or die . . .

A property where incredible and treacherous things start happening around the giant pinnacle of rock, The Reaching Tower, in the center of a vast lake inside the massive estate of Auchenflower. The Reaching Tower – a place where great secrets lie and great mysteries are found – and where the children will ultimately fight and be . . . the ones who fight for the future of everything . . .
