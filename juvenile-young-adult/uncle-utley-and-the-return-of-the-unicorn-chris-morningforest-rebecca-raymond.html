As you may remember from "Uncle Utley's Ugly Underwear", a "U" focused alphabet book, Uncle Utley is very fond of underwear - especially the kind with designs - some ugly and some not. Unfortunately, the local Unicorn also likes underwear - only he EATS it! In this second book, Uncle Utley has purchased two new pair of underwear and he decides that he needs to talk to the Unicorn. He dresses in one pair of new underwear and leaves to find the Unicorn in his lair. He doesn't get far before he realizes that he left the second pair of underwear on his chair. Oh no! Young readers will delight in finding out what happens this time to Uncle Utley in this delightful rhyming book. At the end, Uncle Utley and the Unicorn have an underwear party.
