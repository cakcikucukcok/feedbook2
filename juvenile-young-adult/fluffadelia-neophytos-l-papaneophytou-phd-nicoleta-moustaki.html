Fluffadelia is a friendly and happy feather who one day finds herself traveling a very exciting yet unexpected journey! Her decision to catch the train promises to be uplifting and refreshing, as Fluffadelia ends up high in the blue sky, smiling at clouds, encountering airplanes, and making new friends along the way. The tale of Fluffadelia is a wonderful bedtime story, especially appropriate for younger children, who will rejoice with the fantastic travels of this feathery character. Fluffadelia wishes to inconspicuously instill in our children wonderful communication skills, good manners, great bedtime routines, the values of sharing and caring, as well as the importance of having a wider family circle, including making new great friends. Parents and children will find this heartwarming story to be amusing, colorful, simple, yet with a nurturing underlying plot that will touch parents and children alike. Read Fluffadelia with your children and smile!
