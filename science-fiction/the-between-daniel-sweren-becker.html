The Between is a digital-only novella that follows the shocking events of The Ones and leads directly into its exciting sequel, The Equals.

Having every genetic advantage possible can't save you when you're outnumbered 99 to 1.

James was one of the lucky ones randomly selected before birth to receive genetic engineering. Known as the Ones, this one percent of the population is healthy, beautiful, and talented . . . and to some, that's not fair.

Mounting fear and jealousy of the Ones' success led to the creation of the Equality Movement, which quickly gained enough political traction to demote James, his girlfriend Cody, and others like them to second-class citizens.

Now James has been captured, separated from everyone he knows, and sent to a detention camp. What he finds there will change him into a new kind of revolutionary--if he survives . . .
