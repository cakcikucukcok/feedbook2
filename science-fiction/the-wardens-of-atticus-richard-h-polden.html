Question:

What do you get when you mix the following people together?

• The dysfunctional Milan family — children’s entertainers, whose mission is to find three mythical shards, while saving the galaxy

• An ethically challenged weapon merchant — who will stop at nothing to ensure the Milans do not succeed on their mission

• A bunch of foul smelling giants

• Two law enforcement agents

• A couple of highly trained killers

• An evil, mysterious, ancient race who has the power to destroy them all.

Possible Answers:

A. An intergalactic tale of lust, lost love and forbidden fruit

B. A political thriller explaining in great length the phrase “unfit for purpose”

C. Another bloody book about teenage vampires

D. None of the above.

To discover the answer for yourself, jump aboard and join the party. Will our heroes save the day? Will the weapon merchant triumph? You might even hope the coppers come out on top. Or the trained assassins. Perhaps you’re cheering for the hapless giants. But surely the smart money must be on the mysterious, ancient race of bad arse perpetrators — who are prepared to destroy everything in their path to pull off their wicked enterprise.

It will be refreshing to see the bad guys win for a change, right?
