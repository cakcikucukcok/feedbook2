If youve ever wondered if Albert Einsteins famous E = mc2 theory is true or if the universe was really created from a big bang or about the nature of black holes, then youll want to read this book. Kok Fah Chong explores the latest discoveries in astronomy and physics, including how the spinning-gravitational effects of a star suggest that the universe contains many exoplanetsand he also reveals why that is significant. He examines topics such as how photons can travel across intergalactic distances; why more black holes emerge as the universe expands; why all galaxies will eventually slow down; and how the light of the past universe is catching up with us. He also reveals why all celestial bodies will eventually implode toward the center of the universe, which will result in a scene beyond our imagination. Celebrate the wonders of our universe and stay up to date on some of sciences latest, most interesting discoveries with The Unification Theory.
