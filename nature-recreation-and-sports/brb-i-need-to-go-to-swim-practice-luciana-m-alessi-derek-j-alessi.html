About the Author:

Luciana Alessi

 

Lucy, as she likes to be called, not only loves swimming, but also plays the flute, tennis, and golf.  She also does kids product reviews for toys on YouTube and TV, where she's appeared on dozens of broadcasts.  She has a brother, Luca and sister, Serafina and lives in Clarence, New York.  Her goal is to grow her own swim-ware line, Lucy Swim Wear (Lucyswimwear.com).  She wants to introduce swimming to all kids and is committed to helping teach, mentor and encourage children in adaptive swim programs in her community.  These great programs provide swimming lessons to children with disabilities.
