From The Author Admittedly,I have made numerous mistakes during my long and rewarding career as a condo expert. To say the least, These mistakes have taught me valuable life lessons. I have done my best, therefore, to put these mistakes to pen and discuss them in this Condo Buyer's Crash Course. Since this is a " Crash Course" please note I have limited my writing to what I consider the most important topics for buyers to understand and pitfalls to hopefully avoid. In the guide I often refer to these mistakes, as Red Flags. In my opinion, This buyers guide is an absolute must read if your just starting out in your search for a condo . This guide could also be invaluable if your on a tight schedule and under the gun to make a decision on weather or not to move forward with your condo purchase. I discuss briefly in the guide the pros and cons of condo ownership.
