Job Hunting has changed, and it’s important to know how to market yourself today.  With this book, you will learn the importance of building a portfolio and how to create a proper resume and cover letter.  You will also learn how to organize and properly prepare yourself for the job interview, what the typical interview questions are and how to answer them.  Additionally, learn how to dress and conduct yourself in the interview setting, followed by the proper way to follow-up with the interviewer.

What this book offers that others do not is once you land that job, you need to know how you must work to keep it.  There is an additional section on what you need to do to impress your boss and what it takes to advance within your career field.  Plenty of self-help directions and charts to ensure you are successful.  Finally, there are four basic types of personality and you will learn how to understand them so you can please your boss and learn to work well with co-workers.
