ONLY ONCE HAD HE LET DOWN HIS GUARD…

Years ago, rock-solid mercenary Egan Cassidy had spent a night of sensual ecstacy with Maggie Douglas. Now, their son–a child he never knew existed–was being used as ammunition by a vendetta-seeking madman…and Egan was the target. But running to his son's rescue put him face-to-face with the one woman who'd tempted him into vulnerability. He would have to withstand Maggie's allure this time. He was a soldier of fortune–not a husband. But when Egan delivered mother and child to safety's embrace, could he let his newfound family leave his loving arms?
