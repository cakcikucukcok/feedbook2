Curvy nursing assistant Shelby Watson is blindsided when she loses her job. After an exhaustive job search she finally gets a job taking care of an elderly woman, who happens to be the grandmother of bad boy billionaire Michael Jameson.

The patient is a sweetheart, but he's an arrogant jerk.

If Shelby wasn't facing homelessness, she wouldn't have accepted his job offer, but since she doesn't want to live in her car, she does. She figures that working for a sleazy rich guy is better than living on the streets.

Soon after starting her job, she notices an unusual change in Michael's personality. The once cocky and conceited creep suddenly turns into a nice person.

Why such a change? Is he falling in love with big beautiful Shelby, or is he hiding a heart-pounding secret that could rock them both to the core?
