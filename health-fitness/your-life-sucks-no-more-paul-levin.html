What do you do, when things in your life, must change?  

What do you do, when you are so fed up with the way things are?  

You can give up and pick up apathy, loss of hope and despair.  Or you can demand of the universe your birthright – a happy, joy filled life.  A life where you are the master of your experience.

That’s what happened to me.  I got to the point in my life, where enough was enough.  I was 53. Most of my life I settled for the crumbs.  It was time that I sat at the table and had the lobster. But how?  That’s only for the lucky, the special, the chosen.

And then, to my surprise, I learned that the universe has rules!  When we learn and experience the rules, things in life fall into place.  Decades of being an electrical engineer were paying off.  I reverse engineered the universe.  As soon as I did, I found I wasn’t the first.  In fact, these truths have been around …. forever.  

Allow me to share with you, my experiences with these truths. And the miraculous changes they have brought about in my life.  As I share the things that I have discovered, it is my deepest desire that these truths and the change they bring, will become alive in you.
