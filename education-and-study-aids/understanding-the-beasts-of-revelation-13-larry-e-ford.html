The “Beasts” of Revelation 13 are enigmatic characters that are to appear in a prophetic panorama of the “last days” of our present civilization. There are numerous interpretations about whom or what these characters represent. One concept even says that the second ‘Beast” of Revelation 13:11-18 is none other than the United States! Some ideas are very convincing … if you do not know for what to look.

Larry E. Ford sorts out major details of the scriptural and historical records in order to give you every opportunity to understand this enigmatic prophecy about events that are revealed by God to become the reality of the “last days.” This is a prophetic view of the “last days” unlike most that you will find.
