Bob currently resides in rural Iowa, where he lives with his son, daughter-in-law, and grandson. A Teacher’s Tale chronicles Bob’s life in his first teaching assignment. Bob’s humorous stories from his early life as a teacher provide a fun and inspiring glimpse into the world of education in the 1970’s.
