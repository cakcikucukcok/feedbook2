Ballads from a mother's heart is a book of didactic poems and proses that conveys to you messages of wisdom and morality in the most simplest language, while reading them you will feel like your mother narrating bedtime stories to you or singing to you a lullaby conveying some important lessons for life . The purpose of writing these poems is to convey the message of what our thoughts should be and not simply to depict what reality is around us . The language of the poems is so simple and easy that even a child can enjoy reading the poems although he/she could comprehend it later on in life . Morality is something we are doing away with slowly these days as we are becoming more and more technology oriented we are treating each other like machines ,amidst this chaos we forget that deep inside this robotic body lies a heart that wants to feel the beauty of every passing emotions . It's just a first step towards understanding how beautiful our heart is and how beautiful we can make this world if we understand what our thoughts should be .
