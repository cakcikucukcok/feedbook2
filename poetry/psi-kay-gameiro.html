Thus, along with the frontiers of knowledge, language itself is evolving. The challenge is to push the boundaries of creativity, while as yet restricted by language, to encompass these new philosophical and scientific ideas. This volume of poetry abounds in originality; creatively weaving these ideas into a personal journey.

PSI is the 23rd letter of the Greek alphabet. Its translations include esoteric ideas such as, soul, psyche and the unknown. represents the wave function in quantum mechanics and has meanings in mathematics and the paranormal. Here, it is not only a symbol, but also an acronym, Portal to Symbols and Images. The idea of implies the essence of creativity and the portal we all must pass through as we journey into the deeper mysteries, beyond the reach words, into realms of artistic imagery.
