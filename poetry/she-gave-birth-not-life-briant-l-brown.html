My words and my thoughts equal emotions!

Every time I wrote a poem, these three words were in my equation. I started writing poems in high school after finding a poetry book buried in a dresser drawer in my grandmother's home. The impact and emotions covered my body, and I wanted others to feel that same emotion through my words. This time it will be my vision and my writings!

This journey started just like that buried poetry book. Instead of a book, there were buried relationships that would soon to be discovered. Those relationships of others impacted my emotions, therefore my writings. Some relationships were stronger than others and captured stronger emotions; those stories were easy to convey. I have left out names, a place, or race and revealed a story in a general aspect.

Overall, this book is a passionate journey through the beauty of relationships, love, pain, and most importantly, a reflection of all. We all have a story that we want to share through some form of art. In this book I hope to capture words, thoughts, and emotions that will share a message through my life experiences. In the end of it all, steal your hearts with words.
