The Light of My Life is a collection of fifty beautiful poems based on love and life. Nowadays we are so busy in the commotion of our lives that we have really forgotten the actual meaning and beauty of love. Love within itself is a culture, a tradition, a religion, a life, and a universe. This books aims to decorate your life with the joys of liberty and love. Every poem of this book opens a new chapter of life towards the fragrance of love.This book highlights the value of the unique love that blooms between two common people. Some poems on nature and life have also been composed to help the readers understand the true beauty of life and nature in which they live. In this modern age, these traditional poems will conquer your heart and will make you fall in love with beautiful nature and the love of your life. These poems will reflect the hidden beauty of your heart and will make you feel the unfelt warmth of love. The imagination and admiration of the poet in every poem will compel you to admire the most beautiful things of the world in which you live. We have hidden the glory of this world behind the sadness and stress of our lives. This book aims to unveil that beauty. So here we are with the opening poem of this book, The Light of My Life.
