I thank the LORD my GOD for each one of these poetry. I give him all of the HONOR and GLORY. He has been My all and all, he has given me a second chance at LIFE that is why, I'm thankful and gracious for making any SACRIFICE. I never ever thought about writing Poetry, but being HUMBLE and OBEDIENT, I'm doing GOD WILL In my Life Joyfully. No matter what time of Day or Night when I hear The LORD speak to me, I then put everything to The side and start to write. My true desire is Worshipping the LORD with all My Health and Strength as GOD Bless me SPIRITUALLY, while feeding my SOUL Daily I receive my WISDOM and KNOWLEDGE Through his HOLY WORD peacefully. As GOD delivered me from my worldly life of SIN. The he freely poured out his LOVE upon me Through his GRACE and MERCY, no one or Nothing can ever take his place. My LORD and SAVIOR brought me through In a VICTORIOUS way. That is why no matter Whatever happen he deserve from me all of his HONOR, GLORY, and PRAISE. I'm now CONFIDENT to say that the words that He place within my MIND and HEART, will Be an encouragement to someone to receive A chance for a BRAND NEW START.
