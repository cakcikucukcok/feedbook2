What can you do when the decks are awash and the ship of state is sinking? It’s all around us every day—political criminals and their crony-capitalist backers, outrageous regulation from the White House that spawn hatred and racial strife, socialist lies and left-wing propaganda by a partisan press, plus an insane foreign policy leading us to global nuclear war. God has been kicked off the bus. The poor  farm is back in town. Government schools are teaching Common Core, the Quran, Karl Marx, and one world government.

Most media pundits and the leaders of both parties appear completely unable to comprehend the intense anger millions of Americans feel toward their elected non-representatives. "Against the Wind" is a journey into the gluttonous belly of the political beast. It is a tale of heroism, sacrifice, and honor. It is the story of David versus Goliath. The brave men and women in this book are not politically correct. They fight against evil for God and country. Attract their attention and you may find yourself knocking on heaven’s door.

Larry Henry, Louisville, Tennessee
